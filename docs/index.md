# LayerNexus

**Your all-in-one control center for 3D printing projects.**

Whether you manage one printer or a whole farm, LayerNexus helps you stay organized from the first STL file to the finished print. Upload models, slice them, send G-code to your printers, track filament usage, and estimate costs — all from a single web dashboard.

LayerNexus connects to the tools you already use: [OrcaSlicer](https://github.com/AFKFelix/orca-slicer-api) for slicing, [Klipper/Moonraker](https://github.com/Arksine/moonraker) for printer control, and [Spoolman](https://github.com/Donkie/Spoolman) for filament tracking.

## What Can LayerNexus Do?

- :material-folder-multiple: **Organize Projects** — Group parts into projects and sub-projects with quantity multipliers
- :material-cube-outline: **Preview 3D Models** — Upload STL files and view them right in your browser
- :material-content-cut: **Slice Automatically** — Send STL files to OrcaSlicer and get G-code back without leaving the app
- :material-printer-3d: **Control Your Printers** — Upload G-code and start prints on Klipper printers via Moonraker
- :material-palette-swatch: **Track Filament** — Pick spools from Spoolman and see how much filament each project needs
- :material-format-list-numbered: **Manage a Print Queue** — Line up jobs across multiple printers with priorities
- :material-currency-usd: **Estimate Costs** — See costs per project including filament, electricity, and hardware
- :material-file-document-multiple: **Attach Documents** — Keep PDFs, images, CAD files, and notes with your projects
- :material-wrench: **Track Hardware** — Catalog screws, nuts, electronics, and other non-printed parts
- :material-shield-account: **Team-Friendly** — Admin, Operator, and Designer roles so everyone sees what they need
- :material-theme-light-dark: **Light & Dark Theme** — Looks great on any screen, follows your system preference
